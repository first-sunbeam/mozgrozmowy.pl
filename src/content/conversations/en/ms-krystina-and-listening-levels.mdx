---

## 1️⃣ Ignoring

<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="Minimum energy, quick evacuation. Ms. Krystyna's perfume = overload. This looks like ignoring, but it's emergency survival mode. Otherwise short circuit and smoke." />
<DialogLine speaker="me" text="So she sees lack of respect?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="Yes, and I do this intentionally for her sake. Because a meltdown in front of her would be even worse for her." />

---

## 2️⃣ Fake Listening

<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="Masking deluxe. I nod, murmur mhmm. Inside screaming, but outside a calm mannequin." />
<DialogLine speaker="me" text="But why not tell the truth?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="Because then there would be a lecture about upbringing. I'd rather burn internally than listen to „back in my day…”" />

---

## 3️⃣ Selective Listening

<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="I caught one sentence: „children should…”. The rest floated away in a Chanel No.5 cloud." />
<DialogLine speaker="me" text="So the rest was cut out?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="Yes. Emergency filter. Only hooks, which will still haunt me later at night." />

---

## 4️⃣ Attentive Listening

<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="This is where I feel best. I analyze every word, assemble a map of the conversation. But Ms. Krystyna looks and says: so cold, so without feelings." />
<DialogLine speaker="me" text="Well, that's your favorite mode." />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="Yes. Default. Pity the world prefers smiles and nodding instead of logic." />

---

## 5️⃣ Empathic Listening

<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="This gets hardest. First I must analyze, filter out smell, sounds and chaos. Only then do I catch emotions. But by that time Ms. Krystyna has already taken offense three times." />
<DialogLine speaker="me" text="So your empathy comes later?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="Yes. But when it does arrive, it's real. She just won't live to see it." />

---

## 🔒 Bonus Level: Stimming Level

<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="You won't find this in any model. This is the mode where I fall into analyzing geometric figures, creating patterns… and" />
<DialogLine speaker="me" text="And?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="And drama, because it has to be called dissociation, which means not good. But this isn't ignoring or cutting off from the world — I hear every word and later remember it, only simultaneously freeze in stillness, because inside I'm assembling fractals and moving shapes. From outside it looks like emptiness, but inside I'm rotating a cube in four-dimensional space and counting axis symmetries." />

---

## ✨ Moral?

In reality, the external behavior of an autistic person is not a reliable indicator of whether they are listening and processing information. They may appear fully engaged while simultaneously not retaining content, or conversely - appear absent-minded, yet carefully register every word along with its emotional context.
