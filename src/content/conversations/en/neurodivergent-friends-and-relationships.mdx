## 🫥 Brain in pieces, but still searching

<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="Hey, remember when your friend sent you three frog memes and wrote, 'this is me today'?'" />
<DialogLine speaker="me" text="Of course. And I immediately knew it meant: 'I don't have the energy to talk, but I'm with you with my whole heart (and the whole frog).'" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="Neurodivergent communication — fast, wordless, memetic." />

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="Or how she reminds me every day to eat something, because 'coffee and panic is not a complete meal.'" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="That is love in the ADHD version. A diet based on care, not caffeine." />

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="And that spontaneous DIY neurospinner project? Me: 'Let's start a shop.' Her: 'I'm buying beads wholesale!'" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="One week later: logo, fanpage, and zero products." />
<DialogLine speaker="me" text="But what flow we had! Creativity > logistics." />

<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="A classic: 'Let's throw everything away and run into the forest — no people, just moss, a campfire, and squirrels.'" />
<DialogLine speaker="me" text="I'm still waiting for someone to turn that into a startup." />

<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="And the fact that no one replies for two weeks, and then the conversation returns as if nothing happened?" />
<DialogLine speaker="me" text="That's called a relationship based on trust, not on an algorithm." />

### What does it mean to be a neurodivergent friend?

<DialogLine speaker="brain">
	<p>Neurodivergent friends are not just people. They are:</p>
	<ul>
		<li>walking reminders to “eat something,”</li>
		<li>spiritual support, even when they themselves are in shutdown,</li>
		<li>cheerleaders for your ideas, even the ones that arrive at 3:17 a.m.</li>
	</ul>
</DialogLine>

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="Sometimes I feel that if we all had relationships like this, the world would be less about functioning and more about being together." />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="Wait — what does that mean? Less about functioning?" />

<DialogLine speaker="me">
	<p>You know... the world is always judging you:</p>
	<ul>
		<li>Are you productive?</li>
		<li>Do you reply to emails within two minutes?</li>
		<li>Are you 'on top of things'?</li>
	</ul>
</DialogLine>

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="But with people like this, I feel that I can simply be myself. I don't have to prove that I deserve a place in society by how much I managed to do today." />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="Ah. So it's about... surviving with acceptance instead of with Google Calendar?" />

### Relationships as shelter

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="Exactly. These relationships feel like shelter from a world that measures value in efficiency." />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="So neurodivergent friends are like an emotional-support hot-water bottle — just with memes?" />
<DialogLine speaker="me" text="And sometimes with an evacuation plan into the forest." />
