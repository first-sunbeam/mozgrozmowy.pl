## Not a textbook, but a scene. Not a definition — an experience.

### Act I - Illusion of Control

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="Hey, couldn't we just hyper-focus and get everything done now? Just once in a lifetime, on demand?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain">
	<p>(that is Hyperfocus, in a dramatic voice) Oh, what naivety! You don't choose when I arrive. This isn't “Hyperfocus — activate!”. <strong>It's a curse.</strong></p>
</DialogLine>

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="So I can't summon you when I really need to focus?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="(that is HyperFocus) No, no. That's not how it works. It's more like: 'TADAM! Congratulations! For the next 72 hours your life is a candle-making story. Everything else? Irrelevant.'" />

### Act II - Light and Shadows of Hyperfocus

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="But I had other plans..." />
<DialogLine speaker="brain">
	<p>(with a psychopath's smile) <strong>You don't have plans anymore. You only have candles.</strong> And 48 open tabs in the browser. 🕯️🕯️🕯️</p>
	<p>Everything else has just been moved into the folder called: no longer relevant.</p>
</DialogLine>

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="But at least it will be things I like?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="Sometimes. And sometimes — medieval farming techniques. Or vegetable fermentation. 🥬 Don't ask where it comes from. We just... have to know everything." />

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="So it's not passion. It is...?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain">
	<p>Addiction. To games, rollerblading, crocheting socks 🧶... And today? Creating a world for an RPG campaign.</p>
	<p><strong>And no, we're not going to sleep. This is not the time for sleep.</strong> This is the time for a perfect map with its own ecosystem and mythology.</p>
</DialogLine>

### Act III - 3 AM Again

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="It's 3 AM again..." />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="…so what? I'm watching the fifth tutorial on YouTube right now. 🎬 This is exactly what we should be doing now!" />

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="And if someone talks to me?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="(the great fermentation monologue) Then the great fermentation monologue begins. With charts. With diagrams. With passion. They're already looking for an exit, and we're only just explaining the difference between pickling and lactic fermentation. 🥬" />

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="And if someone interrupts me?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="(furious 😤) INTERRUPT?! I'm comparing salt concentration tables and drawing a diagram of ideal fermentation! And you're asking, ‘What do you want for dinner?’ Dinner is this now! [waving a jar] Eat this! This is all we have." />

### Act IV - After Leaving

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="And then?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain">
	<p>(quietly, dramatically) <strong>Then I disappear. I'm gone.</strong> You're left alone among the ruins. Scrolls. Maps. Notes about the naval war of 1942.</p>
	<p>And you ask yourself: Why do I have seven books on vegetable fermentation? 🥬📚</p>
</DialogLine>

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="But I... already knew almost everything! Almost a master!" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain">
	<p>That means it's time to leave. Poof. I disappear. <strong>And suddenly you don't care at all.</strong></p>
	<p>Those battleships? Dead. Yarn? Indifferent. But wait — now we're learning Swedish while rollerblading. LET'S GO.</p>
</DialogLine>

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="So... where are you now?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="(from behind the garage door) In the lab. I became a fermentation alchemist. 🥬 Pray for the neighbors." />

<DialogLine speaker="me">
	<p>Okay. So hyperfocus is not a superpower you can order on demand, but a state that takes over the whole system.</p>
	<p>It does not give you controllable productivity. It gives you intensity — and often leaves a cost behind.</p>
	<p>And I think that's exactly why the hardest part is that it never arrives when it would actually be convenient.</p>
</DialogLine>
