## 🎭 Not a textbook, but a scene. Not a definition – an experience.

💬 **Dialogue with a brain that knows no pause: "HyperFocus On The Offensive"**

### Act I - Illusion of Control

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="Hey, couldn't we just hyper-focus and get everything done now? Just once in a lifetime, on demand?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="(that is HyperFocus, with dramatic voice) Oh, how charmingly naive! You don't choose when I come. This isn't 'HyperFocus – activate!'. This isn't anime. It's a curse." />

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="So I can't summon you when I really need to focus?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="(that is HyperFocus) No, no. That's not how it works. It's more like: 'TADAM! Congratulations! For the next 72 hours your life is a candle-making story. Everything else? Irrelevant.'" />

### Act II - Light and Shadows of Hyperfocus

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="But I had other plans..." />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="(with psychopath's smile 😈) You don't have plans anymore. You only have candles. And 48 open tabs in the browser. 🕯️🕯️🕯️" />

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="But at least it will be things I like?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="Sometimes. And sometimes – medieval farming techniques. 🌾 Or the life of emperor penguins. 🐧 Don't ask where this comes from. Just... we must know everything." />

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="So it's not passion. It is...?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="Addiction. To games 🎮, rollerblating 🛼, crocheting socks 🧶... And today? Creating a world for an RPG campaign. 🗺️🐉 And no, we're not going to sleep. This isn't time for sleep. This is time for a perfect map with its own ecosystem and mythology." />

### Act III - 3 AM Again

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="It's 3 AM again..." />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="…so what? I'm watching the fifth tutorial on YouTube right now. 🎬 This is exactly what we should be doing now!" />

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="And if someone talks to me?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="(great penguin monologue) Then begins the great penguin monologue. 🐧📊 With charts. With diagrams. With passion. They're already looking for an exit, and we're just at Beyoncé of bird world. Emperor Penguins, baby." />

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="And if someone interrupts me?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="(angry 😤) INTERRUPT?! I'm manually illustrating scrolls with the alphabet of an ancient civilization! 📜✒️ And you ask: 'What do you want for dinner?' Dinner is this now! [waving parchment] Eat this! This is all we have." />

### Act IV - After Leaving

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="And then?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="(quietly, dramatically 🎭) Then I disappear. There's no me. You're left alone among ruins. Scrolls. Maps. Notes about the naval war of 1942. And you ask yourself: Why do I have seven books on vegetable fermentation? 🥬📚" />

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="But I... already knew almost everything! Almost a master!" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="That means it's time to leave. Poof. I disappear. And suddenly you don't care at all. Those battleships? ⚓ Dead. Yarn? Indifferent. But wait – now we're learning Swedish 🇸🇪, rollerblating. LET'S GO." />

<DialogLine speaker="me" text="So... where are you now?" />
<DialogLine speaker="brain" text="(from behind the garage door 🧪🔮) In the laboratory. I became an alchemist. 🧙‍♂️ Pray for your neighbors." />
